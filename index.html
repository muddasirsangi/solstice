Rainy Day

Classic Aloo Pakora

Slice potatoes thin, soak in turmeric water, pat dry.

Whisk chickpea flour, ajwain, and chilli into fluffy batter.

Fry till golden; serve with mint chutney and steaming chai.

Festive

Paneer Corn Fritters

Marinate paneer in hung curd, kasuri methi, and garam masala.

Fold in sweet corn, diced peppers, and besan for texture.

Fry till bubbly edges form; finish with chat masala dust.

View Recipe
Vegan

Spiced Plantain Pakoras

Slice raw bananas, coat with ginger-garlic and lime.

Blend rice flour, besan, and chilli flakes into airy batter.

Deep fry till caramel edges crisp up. Serve with tamarind chutney.

Amritsari Fish Pakora

Coat fish in ginger-garlic, lime, and mustard oil base.

Mix besan, ajwain, and soda water for signature airy crunch.

Fry till double crisp; garnish with pickled onions and chaat masala.
